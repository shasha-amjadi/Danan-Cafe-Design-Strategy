# Typography

Font files can be found in the `/visual-identity/fonts/` directory.

## Overview

Typography plays a central role in expressing Danan's personality.

The typographic system is designed to balance warmth, craftsmanship, readability, and timelessness.

The selected typefaces should support the brand's core attributes:

* Warm
* Crafted
* Timeless

---

## English Typeface

### Bitter

Bitter is the primary English typeface.

Its serif structure conveys:

* Warmth
* Authenticity
* Confidence
* Craftsmanship

Bitter contributes to a timeless visual character that feels approachable rather than corporate.

---

## Persian Typeface

### Nian

Nian is the primary Persian typeface.

Its forms provide:

* High readability
* Contemporary clarity
* Visual harmony with Bitter
* Strong presence across print and digital applications

The combination of Bitter and Nian creates a balanced bilingual typographic system that feels human, refined, and enduring.

---

## Typography Principles

When working with typography:

* Prioritize readability
* Maintain generous spacing
* Avoid excessive font mixing
* Use typographic hierarchy consistently
* Let typography support the content rather than dominate it

Typography should feel calm, intentional, and confident.

---

## Relationship to the Brand

The typographic system should reinforce Danan's visual language by expressing:

* Warmth through humanistic forms
* Craftsmanship through character and detail
* Timelessness through restraint and clarity

Typography should work alongside texture, materiality, and the arch motif to create a cohesive visual identity.

---

## Avoid

* Trend-driven display fonts
* Decorative scripts
* Excessively condensed typefaces
* Overly geometric typefaces
* Excessive font combinations
* Unnecessary typographic effects
* Artificial vintage typography
